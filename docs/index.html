Sports Ratings & Stats

Hobbyist power rankings across basketball, football, soccer, baseball, and hockey that produce ratings that represent points / goals / runs vs. league average. Each sport has a full site with historical and GOAT ratings. Other adds include a similar model for the reality show The Challenge as well as a visualization of tennis Grand Slam championships.

Sports City Index. Each team's rating is standardized to a z-score within its sport (NBA, NFL, MLB, WNBA, NHL, MLS). A city's score combines its teams' z-scores via Stouffer's method: sum_z / sqrt(n). This rewards cities for sustained above-average performance across more teams - a 6-team city averaging +0.5 across the board outscores a 1-team city at +0.5, because the multi-team signal is more reliable. Single-team cities (Stouffer reduces to their team's z-score at n=1) are noted inline. Historical snapshots use each sport's rating closest to Dec 31 of that year.
