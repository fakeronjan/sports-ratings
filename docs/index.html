Sports Ratings & Stats

Hobbyist power rankings across basketball, football, soccer, baseball, and hockey that produce ratings that represent points / goals / runs vs. league average. Each sport has a full site with historical and GOAT ratings. Other adds include a similar model for the reality show The Challenge as well as a visualization of tennis Grand Slam championships.

Cross-Sport Rankings. A rolling view captured at the end of each sport's season. Pick any championship moment to see how cities ranked when ratings are normalized for differences across sports. Cities with multiple teams in the same sport count just their best team in that sport. History goes back to 1980 (the earliest year with NBA, NFL, MLB, and NHL all on board); WNBA and MLS start contributing in 1997 / 1996.
