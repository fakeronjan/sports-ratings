Sports Ratings & Stats

Hobbyist power rankings across basketball, football, soccer, baseball, and hockey that produce ratings that represent points / goals / runs vs. league average. Each sport has a full site with historical and GOAT ratings. Other adds include a similar model for the reality show The Challenge as well as a visualization of tennis Grand Slam championships.

Models + sites built by fakeronjan. Open source on GitHub.
